Vimeo player (captions hosted on Vimeo)

This page features an example of Able Player being used to play a Vimeo video. The video featured is hosted in a Vimeo Pro account, required for hiding the controller and for being able to control playback rate with faster/slower buttons. Captions and subtitles in this demo are stored solely on Vimeo, therefore Able Player is unable to auto-generate an interactive transcript, and users have no control of the appearance of captions.
